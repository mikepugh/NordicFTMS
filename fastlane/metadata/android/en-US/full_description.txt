<p><i>NordicFTMS</i> turns your iFit-enabled NordicTrack or ProForm treadmill, bike, elliptical, or rower into a standard Bluetooth FTMS (Fitness Machine Service) device. Any FTMS-compatible app — such as PowerTread, Zwift, Kinomap, or others — can connect directly over Bluetooth to read live speed, incline, distance, and cadence data, and send control commands (speed, incline, resistance) back to the machine.</p><p>NordicFTMS communicates with the machine hardware via the built-in GlassOS gRPC interface and broadcasts data over BLE FTMS. It auto-starts on boot, auto-detects the machine type, and requires zero configuration. Just install it and go.</p>
